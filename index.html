Reportbase.com is a high-resolution image viewer. It can be integrated into existing applications or used stand-alone.

Tap <here> to load a high-resolution image at a specified x-y coordinate and zoom level. Tap the left side of the image to zoom-out, right side to zoom-in. Long press the image to hide the thumbnail or pinch to resize it. Swipe to load the next image. Load images from the local computer with drag and drop.

Reportbase.com is suitable for the most demanding high-resolution tasks, including: topographic maps, drone and satellite photos, maps, action photography, digital art, panoramas, comics and cartoons, portraits, landscapes, cityscapes, infographics, real estate, automobiles, and vintage paintings.

Contact the deveopers at repba@proton.me for more information.
